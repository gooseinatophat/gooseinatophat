clock

An intuitive, 24h format, timetable clock.
Aimed at those of us who have to deal with ADHD Time-Blindness (or simmilar),
"clock" is designed for optimal processing of visual output in order to help us better plan, establish and navigate our daily schedules and routines.

The "clock" Project started as an atempt at creating something I personally found I needed. During the process it became clear this had the potential to become an essential tool for others as well.
